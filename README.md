Here I have my configurations for my Hyprland and my Waybar,
to be honest, im still a beginner and had a lot of help from AI but nevertheless, I like it!
For me it works and i like the looks of it and they are mostly here so i dont have to do it all over again if something breaks.

I'm thankfull for suggestions if you think you can do it better ;) 

## 🛠️ Requirements & Dependencies

### Core
- **WM:** Hyprland
- **Shell/Bar:** Waybar
- **Terminal:** Kitty
- **Runner:** Rofi-wayland
- **Wallpaper:** Hyprpaper
- **File-Manager:** Thunar

### Fonts
- `ttf-jetbrains-mono-nerd` (Icons & Text)

### Tools & Utilities
- `hyprshot` (Screenshots)
- `wireplumber` & `pavucontrol` (Audio)
- `playerctl` (Media Keys)
- `blueman` (Bluetooth)
- `network-manager-applet` (WLAN)
- `mullvad-vpn` (VPN)
- `lm_sensors` & `libnotify` (CPU Info)
